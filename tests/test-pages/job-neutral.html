Data Analyst

Lakeview Retail Group · Chicago, IL · Full-time · Hybrid

About the Role

Lakeview Retail Group is looking for a Data Analyst to join our merchandising analytics team. You will turn sales, inventory, and customer data into insights that shape buying decisions across our 200+ stores, partnering with category managers and the finance team on weekly and seasonal planning.

Responsibilities

  • Build and maintain dashboards in Tableau for merchandising leadership
  • Write SQL queries against our warehouse to answer ad-hoc business questions
  • Automate recurring reports with Python scripts
  • Present findings to stakeholders in clear, actionable summaries

Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in Statistics, Economics, Business, or a related field
  • 2+ years of experience in an analytics role
  • Strong SQL skills; familiarity with Python or R is a plus
  • Experience with Tableau, Power BI, or a similar BI tool

Benefits

Competitive salary with annual bonus, medical and dental coverage, retirement plan with company match, employee discount, and flexible hybrid scheduling. We are an equal opportunity employer.
