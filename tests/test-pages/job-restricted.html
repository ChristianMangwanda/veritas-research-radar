Systems Engineer, Defense Programs

Meridian Dynamics · Huntsville, AL · Full-time · On-site

About the Role

Meridian Dynamics is seeking a Systems Engineer to support mission-critical defense programs. You will design, integrate, and test embedded systems used in flight hardware, working alongside a multidisciplinary team of engineers across the full program lifecycle from requirements definition through verification.

Responsibilities

  • Develop and maintain system requirements and interface control documents
  • Perform trade studies, failure analyses, and integration testing
  • Coordinate with hardware and software teams on verification plans
  • Support program reviews and customer demonstrations

Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in Electrical, Aerospace, or Systems Engineering
  • 3+ years of experience in systems integration or test engineering
  • Familiarity with MIL-STD documentation and DO-178C is a plus

Eligibility Requirements

US Citizenship is required for this position due to the nature of the work performed under federal contracts. Candidates must be able to obtain a security clearance. No visa sponsorship available for this role, now or in the future.

Benefits

Competitive salary, comprehensive health and dental coverage, 401(k) with company match, and 20 days of paid time off.
