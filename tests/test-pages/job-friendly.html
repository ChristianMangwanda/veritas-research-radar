Research Software Engineer

Helix Biosciences · Cambridge, MA · Full-time · Hybrid

About the Role

Helix Biosciences builds open-source tools for genomics research. As a Research Software Engineer, you will develop scalable data pipelines and analysis tools used by computational biologists around the world, collaborating closely with our science team on single-cell and spatial transcriptomics datasets.

Responsibilities

  • Design and maintain Python and Nextflow pipelines for sequencing data
  • Contribute to our open-source analysis libraries
  • Partner with research scientists to translate methods into production tools
  • Improve performance and reliability of our cloud data platform

Qualifications

  • Degree in Computer Science, Bioinformatics, or related field
  • Experience with Python, cloud computing, and workflow engines
  • Interest in genomics or computational biology

Visa & Immigration

Visa sponsorship is available for this position. We file H-1B petitions for qualified candidates, and OPT/CPT students welcome to apply. International candidates are encouraged to reach out with questions — our immigration team provides support throughout the process.

Benefits

Competitive salary and equity, comprehensive health coverage, relocation assistance, flexible hybrid schedule, and an annual conference budget. We are an equal opportunity employer and value diversity at our company.
